A high-ownership iOS Engineer with a decade of experience leading the 0-to-1 development of
innovative, AI-powered consumer applications. My passion is architecting delightful, high-performance
mobile experiences by bridging the gap between cutting-edge AI, intuitive UX design, and elegant Swift
code. I thrive in fast-paced startup environments, taking on foundational roles to establish best
practices and collaborating across teams to ship products that redefine what's possible with AI on
mobile.

I collaborate with agile teams on research and documentation, UX design, hardware development, and custom fabrication. As an artist, I pursue a passion for natural interfaces combining real-time motion tracking and lighting control. I attended the Wharton School at the University of Pennsylvania as an undergraduate in the Joseph Wharton Scholars Program and hold a graduate degree from the Interactive Telecommunications Program at New York University. I am an Eagle Scout in the Boy Scouts of America.
